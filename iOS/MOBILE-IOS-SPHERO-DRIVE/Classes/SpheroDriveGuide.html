Sphero Drive - User Guide

Sphero Drive is the app you use to cruise your Sphero around your home or office. You have three modes to drive Sphero (Joystick, Tilt and RC) and three speed settings.

 
Aiming
  • Place two fingers on the screen and rotate.
  • An aiming ring will appear.
  • Turn until the blue taillight on Sphero is facing you.
  

Tip: Sphero can be aimed at any time. Try using your thumbs instead of two fingers.
